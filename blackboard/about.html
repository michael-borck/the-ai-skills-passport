▸ About This Initiative

The AI Skills Passport helps staff across the School of Marketing and Management develop practical AI skills they can use in their work. Whether you teach, research, or support the school in a professional capacity, there is something here for you.

The program consists of six standalone learning experiences, a searchable toolkit of guides and frameworks, daily micro-challenges via AI in 5, and optional face-to-face workshops. There are no prerequisites and no fixed order. Start with whatever is most relevant to you.

This initiative was endorsed by the Head of School in late 2025 as part of a broader effort to support staff in understanding and using AI responsibly and effectively.

▸ Content & Copyright

All content within the AI Skills Passport is developed for internal professional development at Curtin University. Materials are authored by Michael Borck with AI assistance and human editorial oversight.

The downloadable guides and frameworks are intended as discussion starters and practical baselines, not definitive references. They reflect current understanding and practice as of early 2026 and will be updated as the field evolves.

Content may be shared within Curtin University for educational purposes. For use outside Curtin, please contact the AI Facilitator.
