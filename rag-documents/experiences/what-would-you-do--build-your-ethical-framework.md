## Build Your Ethical Framework

### Three Questions for Any AI Decision

**1. Transparency**
Would I be comfortable if my AI use were fully visible? Am I being honest about the level of AI involvement?

**2. Understanding**
Can I explain and defend this work without AI? Do I actually understand the content I'm presenting?

**3. Purpose**
Does this use of AI serve the underlying goal? Am I using AI to enhance my work or to avoid doing it?

### The "Billboard Test"

Imagine your AI use was displayed on a billboard outside your building. Would you be comfortable with colleagues, students, and leadership seeing exactly how you used AI? If yes, you're in good ethical territory. If you feel the need to hide or minimize, that's worth examining.
