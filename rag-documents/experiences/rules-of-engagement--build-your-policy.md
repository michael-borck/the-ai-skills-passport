# Rules of Engagement — Build Your Policy

*Experience 3 | Policy & Governance | 30-120 minutes | All roles*

---

Activity 3

## Build Your Policy

Use this framework to draft AI guidelines for your context. Work through each section, considering what makes sense for your specific situation.

1

### Scope & Purpose

Define what this policy covers and why it exists.

Consider:

- Does this apply to all assessments or specific types?

- Does it cover coursework only, or also exams?

- What is the underlying goal? (Skill development? Integrity? Fairness?)

2

### Permitted Uses

What AI uses are explicitly allowed?

Common permitted uses:

Brainstorming
Grammar checking
Finding sources
Explaining concepts
Creating study notes
Code debugging

3

### Required Disclosures

What must people disclose about their AI use?

Disclosure template example:

"If you use AI tools, include a statement describing: (1) Which tools you used, (2) How you used them, (3) What changes you made to any AI output."

4

### Prohibited Uses

What AI uses are explicitly not allowed?

Common prohibitions:

Submitting unedited AI text
Using AI in exams
Undisclosed AI use
AI for individual reflection tasks

Your Draft Policy

Copy this draft and refine it for your specific context. Consider sharing with colleagues for feedback before implementing.
