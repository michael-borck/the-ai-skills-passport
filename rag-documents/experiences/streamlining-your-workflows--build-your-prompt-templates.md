## Build Your Prompt Templates

The most effective AI users don't start from scratch each time. They build a library of go-to prompts tailored to their actual work. Think of these as reusable forms &mdash; the structure stays the same, only the specifics change.

&#128161;

**What makes a good template prompt?**
A good template has blanks you fill in each time &mdash; like a form. Include: what the task is, what context the AI needs, what format you want the output in, and what you should check afterward.

1

### Your most frequent task

Think about something you do every week. Write a reusable prompt that you could paste in each time, filling in just the specifics.

2

### Your most time-consuming task

What task eats hours of your week? Write a prompt that could cut that time significantly.

3

### Your most dreaded task

The one you keep putting off. Sometimes AI removes just enough friction to make you actually start.
