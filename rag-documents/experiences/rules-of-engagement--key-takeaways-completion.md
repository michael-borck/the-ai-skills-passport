# Rules of Engagement — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 3 | Policy & Governance | 30-120 minutes | All roles*

---

## Key Takeaways

**Good policies are clear and contextual**. They tell people what to do, not just what not to do

**No policy is perfect**. Build in mechanisms for edge cases and regular review

**Principles over tool lists**. Focus on transparency and understanding, not specific software

**Communication matters**. The best policy is useless if people don't understand it
