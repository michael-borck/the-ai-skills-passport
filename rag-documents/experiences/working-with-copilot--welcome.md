### What is Microsoft 365 Copilot?

Microsoft 365 Copilot is an AI assistant built into the Office apps you already use: Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Teams. It uses large language models combined with your organisation's data to help you work faster.

**Enterprise vs Consumer: Your data stays private**
Curtin's enterprise Copilot is different from the consumer version. Your data is siloed per user: email queries only see your mailbox, not anyone else's. Copilot can access your SharePoint, shared drives, and personal OneDrive, but only content you already have permission to view. Nothing is used to train the model.

**The mindset for this experience:**
Copilot can do things *for* you, but it is most valuable when it helps you think *better*. Throughout this experience, we will show you both approaches — quick delegation for routine tasks, and conversation for the work that matters. The goal is conversation, not delegation.
