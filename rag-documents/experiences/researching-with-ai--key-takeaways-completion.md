# Researching with AI — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 8 | Research & AI Tools | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

## Key Takeaways

&#10003;
**Conversation, not delegation**. Use AI to challenge your thinking, explore alternatives, and identify gaps — not to produce text you paste in unchanged.

&#10003;
**AI accelerates routine research tasks** (finding papers, generating code, formatting) but cannot replace the intellectual work of analysis and argumentation.

&#10003;
**Always verify AI outputs**. Citations, statistical suggestions, and summaries can contain errors that would undermine your work.

&#10003;
**Disclose AI use transparently**. Check your target journal, funder, and Curtin's guidelines before submitting.

&#10003;
**Protect research data**. Never upload identifiable participant data to public AI tools.
