# AI-Proof Your Assessments — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 4 | Assessment Design | 45-75 minutes | Teaching-focused*

---

## Key Takeaways

**Shift from detection to design**. Create assessments where AI assistance doesn't undermine learning

**Conversation, not delegation**. Design for AI as a thinking partner, and make the conversation itself the evidence of learning

**Transcripts over reflections**. AI writes convincing reflections. Raw conversation transcripts are much harder to fake and show the thinking as it happened

**Three pathways available**: Process documentation, in-class components, and personal application

**Update rubrics**. Value quality of conversation and reasoning over polish
