# Streamlining Your Workflows — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 9 | AI for Professional Staff | 30-60 minutes | Professional staff*

---

## Key Takeaways

&#10003;
**The sweet spot for AI is repetitive, structured, low-risk tasks.** Emails, meeting minutes, formatting, and standard correspondence are where you will see the biggest time savings with the least risk.

&#10003;
**Start with your own content &mdash; AI refines better than it creates.** Write your rough draft, your messy notes, your brain dump. Then let AI help you organise, tighten, and polish.

&#10003;
**Build reusable prompt templates, not one-off prompts.** The real efficiency comes from having go-to prompts you can fill in and reuse, not crafting something new every time.

&#10003;
**Always know what NOT to use AI for.** Confidentiality, judgement calls, and high-stakes communications are yours. AI is a tool, not a substitute for professional responsibility.

&#10003;
**The goal is to free up time for the work that actually needs you.** The less time you spend on mechanical tasks, the more time you have for the thinking, relationships, and problem-solving that make you valuable.
