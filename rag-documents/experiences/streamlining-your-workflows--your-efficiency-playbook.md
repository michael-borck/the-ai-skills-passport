**When NOT to use AI**

- **Confidential data** &mdash; HR matters, student records, or financial data, unless your AI tool is specifically approved for this

- **High-stakes communications** &mdash; where tone nuance matters more than speed (complaints, grievances, sensitive negotiations)

- **Deep understanding required** &mdash; when you need to understand the content deeply, use AI to help you think, not to skip thinking

- **Setup exceeds savings** &mdash; if crafting the prompt takes longer than just doing the task, just do the task
