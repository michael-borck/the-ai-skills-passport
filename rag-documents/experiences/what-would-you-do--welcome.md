# What Would You Do? — Welcome

*Experience 2 | Ethics & Dilemmas | 20-90 minutes | All roles*

---

### By the end of this experience, you will:

- Recognise the ethical complexity of AI decisions

- Examine multiple stakeholder perspectives

- Develop strategies for navigating grey areas

- Articulate your own ethical principles for AI use

### Before we begin...

There's often shame associated with using AI, a feeling that it's somehow "cheating" or undermines authentic work. But AI is a tool, and like any tool, the ethics lie in *how* we use it, not whether we use it at all.

This experience isn't about proving AI is good or bad. It's about developing your judgment for the many situations where the "right" answer isn't obvious.
