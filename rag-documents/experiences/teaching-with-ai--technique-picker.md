## Technique Picker

Beyond basic prompting, these six techniques unlock AI's full potential as a thinking partner. Click each card to explore the technique and see examples.

Board of Directors

Get multiple stakeholder perspectives on any decision or document

Best for: Decisions, planning, reviews

Devil's Advocate

Stress-test your ideas by having AI argue against them

Best for: Testing assumptions, preparing for objections

Reverse Prompting

Let AI interview you first to understand your context

Best for: Complex problems, personalized advice

Assessment Stress-Testing

Discover if your assessment measures what you think it does

Best for: Assessment design, AI-proofing

Chain of Thought

Get better reasoning by asking AI to show its work step by step

Best for: Complex analysis, verifiable reasoning

Few-Shot Learning

Teach AI your preferred style by providing examples

Best for: Consistent output, matching your voice
