## The Detection Game

Can you tell which samples were written by humans and which by AI? Read each text carefully, make your guess, then reveal the answer.

It's important to note that successful marketing strategies now require a multi-channel approach that leverages data analytics and personalization."

## Answer Explanations

**Sample 1:** AI — AI Generated. Note the phrases "It's important to note that..." and "significant transformation" - classic AI markers. Also very balanced and generic.

**Sample 2:** HUMAN — Human Written. Personal frustration, informal contractions, specific details about marks and the writing centre suggest authentic student voice.

**Sample 3:** HUMAN — Human Written. Specific statistical data (r=0.43, p<0.01) and methodological details suggest real research. AI rarely invents convincing statistics.

**Sample 4:** AI — AI Generated. "Multifaceted concept," "fostering open communication," and the perfectly balanced conclusion are typical AI patterns.

**Sample 5:** AI — AI Generated. Despite the casual tone, notice the careful narrative structure and specific sensory details (flat white, corner table, stickers). AI has learned to mimic human storytelling.
