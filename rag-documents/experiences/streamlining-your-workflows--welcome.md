# Streamlining Your Workflows — Welcome

*Experience 9 | AI for Professional Staff | 30-60 minutes | Professional staff*

---

You keep the school running. Every day brings emails to draft, meetings to schedule, reports to compile, data to wrangle, and processes to manage. AI won't replace your judgement about how things work &mdash; but it can take the repetitive friction out of your day.

This experience helps you identify the tasks that eat your time, see how AI can help with the most common ones, and build a personal set of reusable prompts you can start using straight away.

### By the end of this experience, you will:

- Identify repetitive tasks in your daily work that AI can accelerate

- Apply the conversation loop to a real workflow from your role

- Build reusable prompts for your most common tasks

- Know when AI helps and when it gets in the way

- Create a personal AI efficiency playbook
