## Common AI Patterns

While not foolproof, these patterns appear more frequently in AI-generated text. Use them as discussion prompts, not definitive evidence.

**Characteristic phrases**
"It's important to note that...", "In today's rapidly evolving...", "A myriad of factors...", "In conclusion, [restates everything]"

**Overly balanced structure**
Perfect parallel construction, equal treatment of all points, smooth transitions everywhere. Human writing tends to be messier.

**Generic examples**
Examples that could apply anywhere vs. specific local context. "A company" vs. "Bunnings in our case study".

**Consistent voice**
No personality shifts, no frustration, no enthusiasm. Perfectly moderate tone throughout.

**Missing context**
No reference to course materials, lectures, or class discussions. Generic application of concepts without specific unit context.
