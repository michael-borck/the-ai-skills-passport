# The Conversation Loop — Try the Loop

*Experience 6 | Thinking with AI | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

Activity 2

## Try the Loop &mdash; Hands-On Practice

Pick a real task from your work this week. Something you would normally ask AI to help with. Work through the four stages below &mdash; not as prompts to type into a chatbot, but as a thinking exercise.

&#x1F9E0; Stage 1: Brainstorm

Write your real question here. Not a prompt &mdash; your actual problem or question. What are you really trying to figure out?

&#x1F4A1; Stage 2: Ideate

What angles or alternatives would you want to explore? What might you be missing? What would you ask AI to help you think about (not do for you)?

&#x1F504; Stage 3: Iterate

What would you push back on? What constraints, context, or corrections would you add? What does the AI not know about your situation?

&#x1F680; Stage 4: Amplify

How would you make the result yours? What do you know that the AI does not? What would you add, remove, or restructure?

&#x1F914; Reflection

Did your question change as you worked through the stages? Did you notice the gap between what you would have prompted and what you actually need? **That is the loop working.**

Most people find that by Stage 3, they have a much clearer understanding of their own problem &mdash; sometimes clear enough that the AI output matters less than the thinking it prompted.
