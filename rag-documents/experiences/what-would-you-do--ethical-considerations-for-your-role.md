# What Would You Do? — Ethical Considerations for Your Role

*Experience 2 | Ethics & Dilemmas | 20-90 minutes | All roles*

---

In Your Context

## Ethical Considerations for Your Role

### Teaching Dilemmas

**Assessment Design:** How do you design assessments that remain meaningful when AI can generate content?

**Disclosure Requirements:** What level of AI disclosure should you require from students?

**Your Own Use:** Should you disclose to students when you use AI for teaching materials?

**Grading Assistance:** Is it ethical to use AI to help generate feedback on student work?

### Research Dilemmas

**Co-Authorship:** If AI contributes substantially to a paper, should it be acknowledged?

**Data Analysis:** Where's the line between AI as tool and AI as collaborator?

**Literature Reviews:** AI can summarise papers. Is this labor-saving or avoiding scholarly engagement?

**HDR Supervision:** How do you guide students on AI use when practices are evolving?

### Admin Dilemmas

**Decision Making:** What accountability should exist when AI helps with decisions affecting people?

**Communication:** When does efficiency in AI-assisted writing cross into inauthenticity?

**Data Privacy:** What information is appropriate to input into AI tools?

**Policy Development:** How do you maintain human judgment when AI helps draft AI policies?
