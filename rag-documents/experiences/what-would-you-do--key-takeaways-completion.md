# What Would You Do? — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 2 | Ethics & Dilemmas | 20-90 minutes | All roles*

---

## Key Takeaways

✓**Most AI ethics are contextual**. What's appropriate depends on purpose, audience, and expectations

✓**Transparency is usually the right default**. When in doubt, disclose

✓**Understanding matters more than process**. Can you explain and defend your work?

✓**Norms are evolving**. Stay engaged in conversations about best practices
