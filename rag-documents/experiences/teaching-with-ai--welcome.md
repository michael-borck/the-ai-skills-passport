# Working with AI — Welcome

*Experience 5 | Co-Creation & Integration | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

## Welcome

AI isn't here to replace you. It's a collaborator that can amplify your expertise. In this experience, you'll learn practical prompting techniques that transform AI from a novelty into a genuine work partner. You'll build a personal library of prompts tailored to your role, whether you teach, research, or keep the school running.

### By the end of this experience, you will be able to:

- Apply the RTCF framework to write effective prompts

- Use at least three advanced prompting techniques

- Identify which technique works best for different tasks

- Create reusable prompts for your specific work context

- Iterate on AI outputs to get better results
