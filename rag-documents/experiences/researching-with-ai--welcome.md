# Researching with AI — Welcome

*Experience 8 | Research & AI Tools | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

### By the end of this experience, you will:

- Know which AI tools support different stages of the research lifecycle

- Use AI to accelerate literature discovery and gap analysis

- Apply AI writing assistance without losing your academic voice

- Understand how AI can support data analysis and coding tasks

- Navigate disclosure requirements for AI use in research

### AI across the research lifecycle

AI tools are reshaping how researchers find literature, write drafts, analyse data, and prepare publications. This experience walks through each stage, with practical activities you can try using your own research.

The goal is not to automate your research, but to identify where AI saves time on routine tasks so you can focus on the thinking that matters.
