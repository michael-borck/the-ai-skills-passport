# The Conversation Loop — Welcome

*Experience 6 | Thinking with AI | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

You know that delegation feels productive but erodes capability. Hand a task to AI, accept the output, move on &mdash; and somewhere along the way you stop thinking about the problem yourself. This experience teaches you the alternative: a four-stage loop that keeps *you* in the conversation.

The difference between people who use AI well and people who use AI poorly is not the tool. It is whether they stay in the conversation or walk away from it.

### By the end of this experience, you will:

- Distinguish conversation from delegation when working with AI

- Apply the four stages of the conversation loop to a real task

- Recognise when errors compound in unchecked AI chains

- Identify the three cognitive traps that shape how people relate to AI

- Evaluate AI output using proportional scepticism
