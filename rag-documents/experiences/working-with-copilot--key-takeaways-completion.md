# Working with Copilot — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 6 | Microsoft 365 Copilot | 30-60 minutes | All roles*

---

## Key Takeaways

&#10003;
**Conversation, not delegation**. Delegation is fine for routine tasks (summaries, formatting, catch-ups). But for work that carries your name, use Copilot to challenge your thinking, explore alternatives, and sharpen your arguments — then write it yourself.

&#10003;
**Every output needs your judgement**. Copilot can invent details, miss nuance, and flatten context. Review everything before it leaves your desk.

&#10003;
**Your data stays private**. Enterprise Copilot is siloed to your account and does not train the model on your content.

&#10003;
**Be specific in your prompts**. The more context you give (audience, purpose, format), the better the output.

&#10003;
**Start with low-stakes tasks**. Try Copilot on routine emails and summaries first, then work up to more complex documents.
