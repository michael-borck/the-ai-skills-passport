# Is This AI? — Key Takeaways & Completion

*Experience 1 | Detection & Awareness | 15-60 minutes | All roles*

---

## Key Takeaways

**Detection tools are unreliable**. Don't use them as evidence for misconduct decisions

**Patterns suggest, not prove**. Use observations as conversation starters

**Focus on understanding**. Can the person explain and extend their work?

**Design better assessments**. Process-based approaches reveal learning
