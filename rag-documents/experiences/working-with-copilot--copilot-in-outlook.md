## Copilot in Outlook

Copilot in Outlook can draft replies, summarise long email threads, and help you catch up after time away. It works with your mailbox only, so it sees the same emails you do.

### What Copilot can do in Outlook

**Summarise threads**
Open a long email chain and click "Summary by Copilot" to get a concise overview of key points, decisions, and action items.

**Draft replies**
When composing a reply, Copilot can generate a draft based on the thread context. You review, edit, and send.

**Catch up on missed conversations**
After leave or a busy period, ask Copilot to summarise what you missed. It will pull highlights from your unread messages.

**Schedule and organise**
Ask Copilot to find a time for a meeting, draft a calendar invite, or suggest follow-up actions from an email thread.

### Try this prompt

Open a long email thread in Outlook, click the Copilot icon, and try:

"Summarise this thread. List any action items for me and note any deadlines mentioned."

Or when drafting a reply:

"Draft a professional reply confirming I can attend the meeting and asking for the agenda to be shared beforehand."

After returning from leave:

"What are the key emails I need to respond to from last week? Summarise anything urgent."

⚠️

**What to watch for**

- Always read Copilot's draft before sending. It may misinterpret tone or miss nuance.

- Summaries can miss context that was communicated verbally or in separate channels.

- Copilot cannot access emails outside your mailbox, so it may not have the full picture.
